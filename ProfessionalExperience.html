PROFESSIONAL EXPERIENCE

John Deere

During the summer of 2018, I interned with John Deere as an IT intern, working from their Intelligent Solutions Group division. This was an exciting opportunity to get familiar with large scale, enterprise applications.

While at John Deere, I worked with their teams in India to add functionality to one of their most important web applications. The project I worked on helped add convenience and value to 70% of the application users. To accomplish this task, I worked closely with another intern whom I pair programmed with nearly every day. We also utilized the talent around us by asking for help and advice from full time developers, including those in India.

To keep development organized, I also used Agile practices, including pair programming, daily stand ups, retrospectives and test driven development. John Deere as a company heavily relies on these practices, so it was a great place to truly understand the Agile methodology. It also helped me push my communication skills, as over communication was always preferred.

Working with John Deere turned me from a good communicator and Java programmer to a great one. I learned valuable networking skills at several John Deere events and pushed myself to ask for help when I needed it. My Java capabilities were also tested at John Deere, I've never felt better with the language.
